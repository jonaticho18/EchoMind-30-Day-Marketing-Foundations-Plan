EchoMind – 30-Day Marketing Foundations Plan

A focused, ethical, and signal-driven marketing approach designed to establish strong foundations for EchoMind’s next stage of growth.

Candidate: Jonathan Pernia
Role: Digital Marketing Specialist

1. Context & Approach

EchoMind operates a dual-market model:

  • B2B (Architect): A cognitive profiling and reporting platform for organisations and clinicians.
  • B2C (Clinic): Adult neurodivergent individuals seeking clarity and guidance around assessment pathways.

While labelled as B2C in this exercise, the consumer journey is best understood as patient-led demand generation that ultimately supports B2B clinical adoption.

The first 30 days prioritise clarity, learning, and signal generation — not scale.

2. 30-Day Plan

Week 1 – Foundations & Clarity
  • Clarify ICPs and positioning
  • Define value propositions and angles
  • Set up GoHighLevel foundations
  • Configure GA4, UTMs, core events

Not building yet: automation, scaling, complex nurturing.

Week 2 – Funnel MVPs
  • B2C educational self-reflection funnel
  • B2B demo booking funnel
  • Email & SMS confirmations
  • Calendars and forms in GHL
Week 3 – Angle-Based Testing
  • Launch Meta Ads
  • Test 4–5 angles per audience
  • Copy-led creative testing
  • Monitor early performance signals
Week 4 – Review & Optimisation
  • Identify strongest messages
  • Clean and segment lead data
  • Refine funnel friction points
  • Prepare phase-two recommendations

3. Journeys Overview

B2C – Educational Self-Reflection Journey

Purpose: education, segmentation, and demand validation (not diagnosis or sales).

Ad → Educational Landing → Self-Reflection → Whitelist → Educational Follow-up

B2B – Architect Demo Journey

Purpose: generate qualified demo conversations with organisations and clinics.

Ad / Content → Landing → Qualified Form → Demo Booking → Follow-up

4. Success Metrics (Month 1)

  • Landing page conversion rate
  • Cost per qualified lead
  • Booking rate (B2B)
  • Demo show-up rate
  • Email engagement rate
  • Message angle performance (CTR / CVR)

Optional: Budget Context

Indicative test budgets may be used to validate messaging and lead quality. The objective is insight generation, not aggressive acquisition.

This plan intentionally favours focus, ethics, and learning velocity, creating a solid foundation for EchoMind’s next stage of growth.
